About this project

The Zero-Visibility Vision System improves images captured in harsh visual conditions such as fog, smoke and low-light environments. It combines classical image processing techniques with modern enhancement strategies to reconstruct a cleaner, more realistic view of the scene.

Pipeline (simplified)

  • Pre-processing: white balancing & normalization
  • Mode-specific enhancement: dehazing, denoising, or low-light boosting
  • Post-processing: sharpening & contrast refinement for a natural look
